# Houses Reference

The twelve houses divide the chart into life areas. The **cusp sign** of each house colors how that life area is expressed. **Planets inside a house** bring active energy to that domain.

## Angular, Succedent, Cadent

| Group | Houses | Quality |
|-------|--------|---------|
| Angular (strong) | 1, 4, 7, 10 | High impact; planets here are prominent |
| Succedent (stable) | 2, 5, 8, 11 | Consolidating; security and resources |
| Cadent (mutable) | 3, 6, 9, 12 | Transitional; learning and service |

---

## House Meanings

### 1st House — Self / Ascendant
- **Keywords**: appearance, personality mask, first impressions, physical body, new beginnings
- **Cusp sign (Ascendant)**: the mask worn, how others see you at first meeting
- **Planets here**: dominate the personality; easily visible to others
- **Ruling planet of Ascendant**: the *chart ruler* — its placement is especially important

### 2nd House — Resources
- **Keywords**: money, possessions, self-worth, earning ability, material values
- **Cusp sign**: how you earn and value wealth
- **Planets here**: strong influence on finances and self-esteem

### 3rd House — Communication
- **Keywords**: siblings, short travel, writing, speech, early education, local community, mental habits
- **Cusp sign**: communication style
- **Planets here**: active mental life; writers, teachers, communicators

### 4th House — Home & Roots (IC)
- **Keywords**: family of origin, home, private life, emotional foundation, ancestry, end of life
- **Cusp sign (IC)**: emotional bedrock, family atmosphere
- **Planets here**: deeply private; major family influence

### 5th House — Creativity & Pleasure
- **Keywords**: romance (not marriage), children, creative self-expression, play, hobbies, speculation, performance
- **Cusp sign**: how you play and create
- **Planets here**: strong creative or romantic energy

### 6th House — Health & Service
- **Keywords**: daily routines, work environment, physical health, diet, service to others, pets, skills
- **Cusp sign**: approach to daily work and health
- **Planets here**: health matters, work ethic, service orientation

### 7th House — Partnership (Descendant)
- **Keywords**: marriage, business partnerships, open enemies, one-on-one relationships, contracts
- **Cusp sign (Descendant)**: type of partner attracted; qualities projected onto others
- **Planets here**: relationships are central; may attract partners who embody the planet's energy

### 8th House — Transformation
- **Keywords**: shared resources, sexuality, death, inheritance, taxes, other people's money, deep psychology, occult, regeneration
- **Cusp sign**: approach to intimacy and shared power
- **Planets here**: intense; deep psychological transformation

### 9th House — Expansion
- **Keywords**: higher education, philosophy, religion, long travel, publishing, foreign cultures, law, meaning-making
- **Cusp sign**: worldview and quest for meaning
- **Planets here**: philosophical, adventurous, strongly opinionated

### 10th House — Career & Public Life (Midheaven / MC)
- **Keywords**: career, public reputation, ambitions, authority figures, father figure, legacy
- **Cusp sign (Midheaven)**: public persona; ideal career direction
- **Planets here**: highly visible; career and authority are highlighted

### 11th House — Community & Vision
- **Keywords**: friendships, groups, social causes, hopes and wishes, networks, humanitarian ideals
- **Cusp sign**: social style and ideals
- **Planets here**: active social life; strong idealism or group identity

### 12th House — The Hidden Realm
- **Keywords**: isolation, hidden enemies, unconscious, spirituality, self-undoing, institutions, karma, retreat
- **Cusp sign**: area of hidden gifts and fears
- **Planets here**: internalized or hidden; can be a source of unconscious strength or limitation; spiritual depth

---

## Intercepted Houses
When a sign is entirely contained within a house (common in high latitudes), its energy is less accessible — the person must work harder to express it.

## Empty Houses
An empty house is not a deficiency. It simply means that life area flows without crisis — look to the **ruling planet of the house's cusp sign** for more information.
