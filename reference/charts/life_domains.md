# Life Domains & Significators

A reading that stays purely psychological floats. It must land in the rooms where people actually live: the mother, the father, the spouse, money, the body. This file maps chart factors to the concrete people and material domains of a life, and decodes the psychological complexes hidden in aspects.

**Method**: for each relevant domain, combine four evidence sources — (1) the natural significator planet's condition, (2) planets inside the house, (3) the house ruler and *where it went*, (4) tight aspects to any of the above. Then weave the finding into the reading's spine; never output this as a domain-by-domain checklist.

## Significator Table

| Domain | Natural significator | House | Also check |
|--------|---------------------|-------|-----------|
| Mother | Moon | 4th (some schools 10th) | Ceres; loaded Cancer placements |
| Father | Sun, Saturn | 10th (some schools 4th) | |
| Siblings | Mercury | 3rd | |
| Spouse & marriage | Venus (love), Juno (the vow), 7th ruler | 7th | Descendant sign; planets in 7th |
| Romance & children | Venus, Jupiter | 5th | 5th ruler |
| Earned wealth | Venus, Jupiter, 2nd ruler | 2nd | planets in 2nd |
| Others' money / inheritance / debt | Pluto, 8th ruler | 8th | Ceres or Node contacts to 8th bodies |
| Health & body | ASC + 1st (vitality), 6th (illness, routine) | 1st, 6th, 12th (hidden/chronic, hospitals) | malefic hard aspects into these houses |
| Career & public standing | MC sign, 10th ruler, Sun/Saturn | 10th | 6th for daily work style |

**When parental houses conflict with the significator, trust the significator + tight aspects**: the Moon describes the mother *as the child experienced her* — her emotional weather, not her résumé. Sun/Saturn describe the father-experience the same way. A planet's hard aspects describe the *relationship*, not the parent's objective character — say it that way.

**House-ruler displacement is a sentence.** "Ruler of X in Y" means the affairs of X live in the room of Y:
- 2nd ruler in 8th → income flows through other people's resources (partner's money, investment, insurance, inheritance, managing others' assets)
- 7th ruler in 6th → the spouse is met through work or daily routine
- 10th ruler in 9th → the career lives abroad, in academia, in publishing, in belief systems
- Apply the same grammar to any pair.

## Timing & Condition Signatures

| Signature | Concrete reading |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Saturn in 7th | Marriage delayed or deliberately slow; partner older, serious, or met in maturity; once built, unusually durable |
| Saturn in own sign / exalted | The delay is construction, not denial — the domain solidifies with age |
| Venus retrograde | Love matures late; first loves feel unfinished; past people return; the person re-evaluates what love is before it works |
| Ruler retrograde in a domain | The domain develops on a second attempt — reworked, not blocked |
| Malefic in fall/detriment in a house | That domain's engine misfires under stress — name the specific malfunction |
| Benefic dignified in a house | Genuine protection there — name what flows easily |

## Health Mapping (brief)

Sign on 6th/1st and planets there point to the body's weak lines:

- **Cardinal signs**: Aries head/inflammation · Cancer stomach/digestion/chest · Libra kidneys/lower back · Capricorn bones/knees/skin/teeth
- **Fixed**: Taurus throat/thyroid · Leo heart/spine · Scorpio reproductive/elimination · Aquarius circulation/ankles/nerves
- **Mutable**: Gemini lungs/nerves/hands · Virgo intestines/anxiety-gut axis · Sagittarius liver/hips · Pisces immune/lymph/feet, substance sensitivity

Planet overlays: Mars = inflammation and acute flare; Saturn = chronic, restriction, deficiency; Uranus = nerves, spasms, sudden onset; Neptune = fatigue, immunity, misdiagnosis, substance sensitivity; Moon in 12th = sleep and somatization. A malefic *opposing* into the 6th from the 12th links illness to suppressed emotion — say which emotion, from the aspect.

Keep health language observational, never diagnostic: "this chart's stress tends to land in ___" — and always with the behavioral lever that relieves it.

## Psychological Complexes — the aspect's hidden layer

This is what the person never says out loud, and what a deep reading is *for*. Read hard contacts between personal planets and parental/marital significators as complexes — the childhood adaptation still running in adult relationships:

| Pattern | Complex reading |
|---------|----------------|
| Dominant Moon / Moon conj ASC | Identity fused with the mother; her moods were the household weather and became the person's inner weather. With supporting Cancer placements this is a full mother-complex (恋母) structure: the person reads every room the way they once read her face |
| Mars in Cancer, or Mars–Moon hard | Anger *toward* mother/home that was never permitted; converted into guilt, passive aggression, or bodily tension at home |
| Juno or Venus in Cancer, or hard to Moon | Seeking the mother in the partner — wanting to be fed, held, anticipated — then fighting the partner for the freedom the mother never gave |
| Sun–Saturn hard (any orb if exact-ish) | The father's unspoken judgment internalized as the inner critic; achievement becomes a lifelong plea for an approval that never quite arrives |
| Sun in 9th or 12th, weakly aspected | Father experienced as distant, absent, or admired from afar — the person builds an idealized inner father and measures themselves against him |
| Moon–Saturn hard | Emotional rationing: learned early that needs are a burden; loves by enduring |
| Venus–Pluto hard | Love entangled with control; intimacy triggers the family's power grammar — jealousy, testing, all-or-nothing |
| Venus–Neptune hard | Loves the dream of the person; disillusionment cycle until the fantasy is owned |
| Pluto in 4th | One parent ruled the home invisibly; power is the family's unspoken language, and the person is hypersensitive to control forever after |
| Saturn 7th vs Pluto 4th (hard) | The marriage must renegotiate the family-of-origin's power rules; in-law or loyalty triangles likely until the person openly chooses their own house's law |
| 2nd ruler in 8th, or Venus–8th contacts | Money entangled with intimacy: inheritance, the spouse's resources, joint finance — and the fear of dependency that comes with it |

**Rules for using this table:**
1. State the complex plainly — soft-pedaling ("你可能有时会...") wastes it. But frame as *pattern, not verdict*.
2. Tie it to the exact contact and orb ("婚神星刑金星，紧到 0.7°").
3. Trace the arc: childhood adaptation → present cost → the adult choice that rewrites it. A complex named without the exit is just an accusation.
4. Two or three complexes that converge on the spine beat six listed separately.
