# Zodiac Signs Reference

## Elements & Modes

| Element | Signs | Core Energy |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| Fire | Aries, Leo, Sagittarius | Action, inspiration, identity |
| Earth | Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn | Stability, practicality, material |
| Air | Gemini, Libra, Aquarius | Communication, intellect, connection |
| Water | Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces | Emotion, intuition, depth |

| Mode | Signs | Core Drive |
|------|-------|------------|
| Cardinal | Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn | Initiate, begin, lead |
| Fixed | Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius | Sustain, stabilize, persist |
| Mutable | Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces | Adapt, transition, synthesize |

---

## Sign Profiles

### ♈ Aries (Mar 21 – Apr 19)
- **Ruler**: Mars · **Element**: Fire · **Mode**: Cardinal
- **Keywords**: pioneering, bold, impulsive, competitive, self-starting
- **Strength**: courage, directness, initiative
- **Shadow**: impatience, aggression, self-centeredness
- **Life theme**: "I am" — self-assertion and new beginnings

### ♉ Taurus (Apr 20 – May 20)
- **Ruler**: Venus · **Element**: Earth · **Mode**: Fixed
- **Keywords**: sensual, patient, reliable, possessive, pleasure-seeking
- **Strength**: endurance, loyalty, appreciation of beauty
- **Shadow**: stubbornness, materialism, resistance to change
- **Life theme**: "I have" — values, security, sensory experience

### ♊ Gemini (May 21 – Jun 20)
- **Ruler**: Mercury · **Element**: Air · **Mode**: Mutable
- **Keywords**: curious, versatile, communicative, restless, dual-natured
- **Strength**: adaptability, wit, learning speed
- **Shadow**: superficiality, inconsistency, anxiety
- **Life theme**: "I think" — ideas, language, connection

### ♋ Cancer (Jun 21 – Jul 22)
- **Ruler**: Moon · **Element**: Water · **Mode**: Cardinal
- **Keywords**: nurturing, intuitive, protective, moody, home-oriented
- **Strength**: empathy, memory, emotional intelligence
- **Shadow**: clinginess, defensiveness, emotional manipulation
- **Life theme**: "I feel" — family, roots, emotional security

### ♌ Leo (Jul 23 – Aug 22)
- **Ruler**: Sun · **Element**: Fire · **Mode**: Fixed
- **Keywords**: creative, generous, dramatic, proud, loyal
- **Strength**: leadership, warmth, self-expression
- **Shadow**: arrogance, need for validation, inflexibility
- **Life theme**: "I will" — self-expression, creativity, recognition

### ♍ Virgo (Aug 23 – Sep 22)
- **Ruler**: Mercury · **Element**: Earth · **Mode**: Mutable
- **Keywords**: analytical, service-oriented, precise, critical, health-conscious
- **Strength**: discernment, reliability, practical intelligence
- **Shadow**: perfectionism, over-criticism, worry
- **Life theme**: "I analyze" — refinement, service, health

### ♎ Libra (Sep 23 – Oct 22)
- **Ruler**: Venus · **Element**: Air · **Mode**: Cardinal
- **Keywords**: diplomatic, relationship-focused, aesthetic, indecisive, fair
- **Strength**: mediation, charm, sense of justice
- **Shadow**: people-pleasing, avoidance of conflict, codependency
- **Life theme**: "I balance" — partnership, harmony, beauty

### ♏ Scorpio (Oct 23 – Nov 21)
- **Ruler**: Pluto (traditional: Mars) · **Element**: Water · **Mode**: Fixed
- **Keywords**: intense, transformative, investigative, secretive, powerful
- **Strength**: depth, resilience, insight into hidden truths
- **Shadow**: jealousy, manipulation, obsession
- **Life theme**: "I desire" — power, transformation, intimacy

### ♐ Sagittarius (Nov 22 – Dec 21)
- **Ruler**: Jupiter · **Element**: Fire · **Mode**: Mutable
- **Keywords**: adventurous, philosophical, optimistic, blunt, freedom-loving
- **Strength**: vision, generosity, quest for truth
- **Shadow**: restlessness, dogmatism, lack of follow-through
- **Life theme**: "I seek" — expansion, wisdom, higher meaning

### ♑ Capricorn (Dec 22 – Jan 19)
- **Ruler**: Saturn · **Element**: Earth · **Mode**: Cardinal
- **Keywords**: disciplined, ambitious, responsible, reserved, strategic
- **Strength**: perseverance, leadership, long-term planning
- **Shadow**: coldness, rigidity, workaholism
- **Life theme**: "I use" — achievement, structure, mastery

### ♒ Aquarius (Jan 20 – Feb 18)
- **Ruler**: Uranus (traditional: Saturn) · **Element**: Air · **Mode**: Fixed
- **Keywords**: progressive, humanitarian, unconventional, detached, inventive
- **Strength**: originality, social vision, intellectual freedom
- **Shadow**: emotional distance, rebellion for its own sake, aloofness
- **Life theme**: "I know" — innovation, community, the future

### ♓ Pisces (Feb 19 – Mar 20)
- **Ruler**: Neptune (traditional: Jupiter) · **Element**: Water · **Mode**: Mutable
- **Keywords**: compassionate, intuitive, dreamy, self-sacrificing, spiritual
- **Strength**: creativity, empathy, spiritual depth
- **Shadow**: escapism, victimhood, lack of boundaries
- **Life theme**: "I believe" — dissolution of ego, transcendence, compassion

| Pair | Tension |

| Taurus ↔ Scorpio | Possession vs. Transformation |
| Gemini ↔ Sagittarius | Facts vs. Meaning |
| Cancer ↔ Capricorn | Home vs. Career |
| Leo ↔ Aquarius | Individual vs. Collective |
| Virgo ↔ Pisces | Analysis vs. Surrender |
