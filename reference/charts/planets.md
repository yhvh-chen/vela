## Additional Points Currently Supported

These are not part of the classical ten planets, but they are valid chart anchors in this runtime and should be used when present.

| Point | Symbol | Core Principle |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| North Node | ☊ | Growth direction, unfamiliar development, evolutionary pull |
| South Node | ☋ | Old mastery, comfort zone, inherited pattern, overused reflex |
| Chiron | ⚷ | Core wound, compensatory defense, healing intelligence |

- Treat the Nodes as an **axis**. They must be read together.
- Treat Chiron as a wound-to-wisdom pattern: what hurts, how the person protects against it, and what mature integration looks like.
- Do **not** invent unsupported asteroid placements. If an asteroid is absent from chart data, leave it out.

## Planet in Sign — Interpretive Notes

### ☉ Sun (Identity & Purpose)
- **In Fire signs** (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius): expressive, action-oriented self; needs to create and lead
- **In Earth signs** (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn): grounded identity; purpose tied to tangible achievement
- **In Air signs** (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius): intellectual self; purpose through ideas and relationships
- **In Water signs** (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces): emotionally defined identity; purpose through depth and feeling

### ☽ Moon (Emotional Needs)
- **Aries**: needs autonomy and quick emotional release; reactive
- **Taurus**: needs stability, comfort, sensory reassurance; stubborn when threatened
- **Gemini**: needs variety and mental stimulation; emotionally changeable
- **Cancer**: needs security and deep belonging; highly nurturing
- **Leo**: needs appreciation and creative expression; dramatic emotions
- **Virgo**: needs order and usefulness; anxiety-prone
- **Libra**: needs harmony and fairness; avoids emotional conflict
- **Scorpio**: needs intensity and total trust; deeply private
- **Sagittarius**: needs freedom and optimism; avoids being pinned down emotionally
- **Capricorn**: needs control and achievement; suppresses vulnerability
- **Aquarius**: needs independence and intellectual connection; detaches from feeling
- **Pisces**: needs empathy and transcendence; highly absorptive emotionally

### ☿ Mercury (Mind & Communication)
- Indicates *how* the person thinks and communicates
- **Retrograde Mercury**: introspective thinking; may be slow to express; misunderstood; powerful inner dialogue
- **In same sign as Sun**: highly identified with their own perspective
- **In adjacent sign to Sun**: can think in ways the ego resists

### ♀ Venus (Love & Values)
- Indicates what the person finds beautiful, how they attract, what they value in relationships
- **Venus in Aries/Scorpio**: passionate, direct, can be combative in love
- **Venus in Taurus/Libra**: comfort-seeking, sensual, needs reciprocity
- **Venus in Virgo**: shows love through acts of service; self-critical in relationships

### ♂ Mars (Drive & Action)
- Indicates how the person asserts themselves and pursues desire
- **Retrograde Mars**: internalized drive; may feel frustrated acting outwardly; powerful but delayed
- **Mars in Water signs**: driven by emotion; can be passive-aggressive
- **Mars in Fire signs**: direct, energetic, can burn out quickly

### ♃ Jupiter (Growth & Luck)
- House placement shows where life expands most naturally
- Sign shows the style of growth and where generosity lies

### ♄ Saturn (Discipline & Karma)
- House placement shows life's primary lesson and area of restriction/mastery
- **Saturn Return** (~28-30, ~58-60): major life restructuring periods

### ⚷ Chiron (Wound & Repair)
- House placement shows the life area where shame, fracture, or "never enough" themes recur
- Sign shows the style of hurt and the style of healing
- Mature Chiron expression often becomes guidance, craft, or sensitivity offered to others

### ♅ Uranus (Disruption & Innovation)
- Generational planet; house placement personalizes its influence
- Shows where the person rebels, innovates, or experiences sudden change

### ♆ Neptune (Dreams & Dissolution)
- Generational planet; house placement shows where idealism or illusion operates
- Shows where boundaries blur; spiritual gifts and self-deception

### ♇ Pluto (Transformation & Power)
- Generational planet; house placement shows where deep transformation occurs
- Shows compulsions, power struggles, and ultimate regeneration

## Retrograde Planets

A retrograde planet's energy turns inward. Effects by planet:

| Planet | Retrograde Effect |
|--------|-------------------|
| Mercury | Inward thinking, review, communication delays |
| Venus | Revisiting past relationships; deeper but slower intimacy |
| Mars | Internalized drive; frustration before breakthrough |
| Jupiter | Blessings come through inner work, not outer luck |
| Saturn | Lessons from past lives or early life; severe self-discipline |

## Node Axis — Quick Reading Rule

- **South Node**: what is easy, familiar, and tempting to overuse
- **North Node**: what feels less natural at first, but opens the life forward
- Use symbolic language: "old pattern," "carried habit," "familiar strategy," not literal certainty claims

| Neptune | 12 | Spiritual depth, hidden realms |
